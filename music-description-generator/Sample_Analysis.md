## ðŸ” Analysis Interpretation Guide

### Energy Levels
- **0-30%**: Very calm, ambient, background
- **31-50%**: Relaxed, chill, downtempo
- **51-70%**: Moderate, balanced, versatile
- **71-85%**: Energetic, upbeat, engaging
- **86-100%**: Very high energy, intense, driving

### Danceability
- **0-40%**: Not suitable for dancing
- **41-60%**: Moderate groove, some movement
- **61-80%**: Good dance potential
- **81-100%**: Highly danceable, club-ready

### Valence (Positivity)
- **0-30%**: Dark, sad, melancholic
- **31-50%**: Neutral, balanced
- **51-70%**: Generally positive
- **71-85%**: Very positive, uplifting
- **86-100%**: Extremely happy, euphoric
